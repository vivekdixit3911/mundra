What is EPR Registration?

Under the Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) framework, businesses involved in manufacturing, importing, or selling products such as plastics, electronics, and batteries must obtain EPR Registration. It ensures responsible waste management, regulatory compliance, and contributes to environmental sustainability.

Documents Required for EPR Registration

PAN Card & Aadhaar of Applicant / Directors / Partners

Proof of Business Address (Electricity Bill / Rent Agreement / Property Papers)

GST Registration Certificate

Partnership Deed / Incorporation Certificate / LLP Agreement

Shop/Trade Licence (if applicable)

No Criminal Record Declaration (as per EPR compliance)

Affidavit / Undertaking for EPR Compliance
