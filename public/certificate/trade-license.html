What is a Trade License?

A Trade License is a legal document issued by the local municipal authority that permits businesses, shops, and establishments to operate lawfully. It ensures that your business complies with safety, health, and regulatory standards, protecting both owners and customers.

Benefits of Having a Trade License

  • Mandatory for businesses to operate legally under municipal laws
  • Builds credibility and trust with customers
  • Ensures compliance with safety, health, and regulatory standards
  • Helps avoid penalties, legal issues, or business closure
  • Essential for applying for loans, tenders, and government benefits
