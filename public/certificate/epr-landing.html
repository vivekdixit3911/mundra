Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is EPR Certificate and who needs it?

EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) Certificate is mandatory for all manufacturers, importers, and brand owners dealing with plastic packaging, e-waste, batteries, tires, and packaging materials. It's required under MoEFCC regulations to ensure proper waste management.

Q2: How long does EPR registration take?

With Mundra Legal, EPR registration takes 7 working days from document submission. Traditional process can take 30-45 days. We expedite through expert documentation and direct CPCB liaison.

Q3: What is the validity of EPR Certificate?

EPR Certificate is valid for 5 years from date of issuance. Annual compliance returns must be filed every year. We provide free annual compliance support with our package.

Q4: What are penalties for not having EPR Certificate?

Operating without EPR Certificate can result in penalties up to ₹1 Crore, business closure, criminal liability under Environment Protection Act, and product recall orders. Immediate registration is recommended.

Q5: Can I get EPR for multiple product categories?

Yes, you can apply for EPR across multiple categories (Plastic, E-waste, Battery, Tire, Packaging) simultaneously. We offer combo packages with discounted pricing for multiple registrations.

Q6: Is EPR different from pollution clearance?

Yes, EPR is separate from Consent to Establish/Operate. EPR focuses on post-consumer waste management responsibility, while pollution clearance covers operational emissions. Both may be required depending on business type.

Q7: Do small businesses need EPR Certificate?

Yes, EPR is mandatory for all businesses regardless of size if they produce, import or sell notified products. Threshold limits vary by category - consult our experts for your specific case.

Q8: Can I apply for EPR online?

Yes, EPR registration is done through CPCB's online portal. However, expert guidance ensures proper documentation, classification, and faster approval. We handle entire online process for you.
