What is WPC Certificate?

The WPC Certificate, issued by the Wireless Planning & Coordination Wing of the Department of Telecommunications (DoT), is mandatory for importing or selling wireless, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi-enabled devices in India. It ensures that all wireless equipment complies with Indian frequency and technical standards.
